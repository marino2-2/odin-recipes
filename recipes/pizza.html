Margherita Pizza Recipe

Description

Savor the classic charm of our Margherita Pizza, where artisanal pizza dough provides the perfect canvas for a symphony of flavors. A savory blend of crushed tomatoes, garlic, and herbs forms the base, complemented by the creamy embrace of fresh mozzarella and the fragrant essence of basil. Baked to perfection, the golden crust offers a delightful crunch, while a finishing drizzle of extra virgin olive oil adds a touch of richness. This culinary masterpiece encapsulates the essence of Italian tradition — a celebration of simplicity, quality, and the timeless comfort found in each delicious slice.

Ingredients

Dough ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110°F/43°C)
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Pizza sauce

  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Topping

  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil (for drizzling)

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough

    1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until the dough starts to come together.
    3. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

  2. Prepare the pizza sauce

    1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the crushed tomatoes, minced garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
    2. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

  3. Preheat the oven

    • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 475°F/245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.

  4. Roll out the dough

    • Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to a floured surface. Roll it out into your desired pizza shape and thickness.

  5. Assemble the pizza

    1. If you have a pizza peel, transfer the rolled-out dough onto it. If not, use parchment paper on a baking sheet.
    2. Spread a thin layer of the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    3. Arrange the sliced mozzarella on top of the sauce. Add fresh basil leaves.

  6. Bake the pizza

    1. If you're using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the pizza from the peel to the hot stone in the oven. If using a baking sheet, place it directly in the oven.
    2. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  7. Finish and serve

    1. Remove the pizza from the oven and drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.
    2. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!
